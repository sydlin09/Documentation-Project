Stuffed Animal Repair

Use this procedure when repairing an opening in a stuffed animal.
  1. Hide the knot inside the opening.
  2. Insert the needle through one folded edge.
  3. Move directly across to the opposite folded edge.
  4. Continue sewing back and forth across the opening.
  5. Pull the thread gently to close the gap.
The opening closes with minimal visible stitching.
