Seam Hole Repair

Use this procedure when a seam has opened but the fabric is not missing.
  1. Flip the item inside out.
  2. Align the fabric edges.
  3. Insert the needle slightly before the opening.
  4. Sew along the original seam using a backstitch.
  5. Continue until the opening is fully closed.
The seam is securely repaired and matches the original stitching.
