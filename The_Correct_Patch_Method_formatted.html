The Correct Patch Method

Learn the differences between common patch types.

Iron-on:

  • Iron-on: Quick and easy to apply.

  • However, they may loosen over time and can damage delicate fabrics when heat is used.

  • Do not use iron-on patches on stuffed animals or fabrics that may melt when exposed to heat.

Sew-on :

  • Sew-on patches provide the strongest and most durable repair.

  • They are recommended for frequently used clothing and sentimental items.

Warning:

  • Do not use iron-on patches on stuffed animals or fabrics that may melt when exposed to heat.
