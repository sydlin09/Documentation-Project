How to Prepare Needle and Thread

Materials Needed: • Hand-sewing needle • Thread (Matching Color) • Scissors • Optional Needle threader
  1. Cut about 18 inches of thread.
  2. Insert the thread through the needle eye.
  3. Grab both ends of the thread so that it will side by side by side.
  4. Pull the thread until both ends are even and far away from the eye of the needle.
  5. Wrap the two ends of thread around your finger to make a small circle
  6. Insert the two ends inside the circle to make a knot. (This is considered a double knot)

The needle is threaded and ready for sewing.
