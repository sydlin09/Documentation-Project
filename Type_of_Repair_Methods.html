Type of Repair Methods

Choose the repair method that best matches the type of damage.

Direct Sewing:

  • Use direct sewing when the edges of the tear still meet together.

  • This method works well for split seams, small tears, and stuffed animals.

Patching:

  • Use a patch when fabric is missing or badly damaged.

  • Patches provide extra support and work especially well for jeans and other high-wear items.

  • Use this for large holes where fabric is missing, especially in high-wear areas like denim.
