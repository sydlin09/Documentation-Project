Attaching an External Patch

• Fabric patch • Needle and thread • Pins
  1. Cut a patch that extends at least one-half (1/2) inch beyond the damaged area on all sides.
  2. Place the patch over the hole.
  3. Pin the patch in place.
  4. Sew around the patch edge using a back stitch.
  5. Bring the needle up through the fabric.
  6. Insert the needle one stitch length behind your starting point. Bring the needle back up one stitch length ahead.
  7. Bring the needle back up one stitch length ahead.
  8. Continue sewing by repeating these steps to create a strong line of stitches.
  9. Remove the pins.
The hole is covered and reinforced by the patch.
